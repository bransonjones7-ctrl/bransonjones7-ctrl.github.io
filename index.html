Experience

What I actually do at EIG

Supporting the CFO of a Monroeville, PA firm means the work is real, recurring, and reviewed. A sample of it:

  • Built complete financial statements, from the income statement through the statement of cash flows, out of public Form 990 filings for a fourteen-organization peer benchmarking study
  • Constructed ratio scorecards covering profitability, liquidity, leverage, and operating performance for board presentation
  • Perform monthly credit card, vision, and dental reconciliations across operational departments
  • Maintain direct care hours and wage analyses that feed leadership planning
  • Prepare variance analyses and ad hoc Excel summaries for executive decisions
Featured project

Tillbrook

Tillbrook started with an uncomfortable observation about my own job. A meaningful share of the work I do professionally, reconciliations, reporting packets, data pulls, and formatting, is exactly the kind of work AI can now handle with human oversight.

An owner comparing roughly $2,000 a month for an automated reporting system against $70,000 or more a year, all-in, for a junior analyst is not making a hard decision. I would rather be the one building the system than the one waiting to be compared to it.

Tillbrook pairs AI-driven workflow automation with the financial analysis I already do professionally. The full site is in development.
